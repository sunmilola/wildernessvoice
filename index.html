About the Ministry

Voice in the Wilderness Ministries exists to help believers know God deeply and live from His love. What began as The Berean Adventure: A Journey into the Heart of God—rooted in Ephesians 1:17–19—invited readers to seek revelation and intimacy with the Father. Step by step, that pursuit grew into The Caritas Experience, a call to respond to love received with love returned.

From knowing to loving, from information to transformation, the focus remains clear: foster intimacy with God through worship, prayer, and the Word—forming disciples who live each day from the fullness of His presence and grace.

The Caritas Experience

From Love Received to Love Returned. A transformational discipleship journey helping believers move from knowing God’s love to loving Him back with their whole lives.

40-day Manifesto His Story • The Way • Loving God

Objective

Close the gap between salvation and surrender; belief and belonging; being saved from and being saved for.

  • Rediscover the goal — God desires love, not performance.
  • Remember the reason — we love because He first loved us.
  • Relearn the way — daily rhythms of intimacy, obedience, joy.
  • Reignite the heart — first love by grace, not guilt.
  • Reproduce the life — mentor and multiply others.

Author

Minister • Teacher • Spiritual Mentor

Oladele Adeleye

Oladele’s passion is simple: to help people walk in the fullness of what they’ve received in Christ. His writing blends prophetic insight with pastoral care, inviting readers to encounter God personally and live from His love.

For seven years he served in leadership with Bible Study Fellowship (BSF), teaching Scripture and mentoring men in the San Francisco class — a class he helped revive after decades of dormancy, and which continues to flourish today. (Shared here for biographical context; BSF does not endorse external ministries.)

He holds a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Chemistry, a Postgraduate Diploma in Management Studies, and later earned a Bachelor of Divinity, deepening his theological understanding and lifelong commitment to Christian ministry.

Statement of Faith

  • Scripture: The Bible is God-breathed, our final authority for faith and life.
  • God: One God in three Persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ: Fully God and fully man; His death and resurrection secure our salvation.
  • Salvation: By grace through faith, unto new life and lifelong discipleship.
  • The Church: Called to love God and neighbor, making disciples of all nations.
